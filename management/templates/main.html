Quick Start Guide

Go to the Members section and click "Add New Member". Fill in their details including name, contact information, and join date. You can also add referrals.

Navigate to Facilities and click "Add New Facility". Set the facility name, member cost, guest cost, and monthly maintenance fees.

Go to Bookings and click "New Booking". Select a member, facility, start time, and number of slots. Confirm to save the booking.

Tips & Tricks
  • Sort your members by join date to find recent signups
  • Set competitive pricing for different membership tiers
  • Monitor bookings to track facility usage and demand
  • Track referrals using the recommendation feature
  • Review maintenance costs regularly for profitability
